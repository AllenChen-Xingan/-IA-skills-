# Quality Sources for Expert Identification

Comprehensive guide to authoritative sources for identifying top scholars, journals, and research materials across all disciplines.

## 1. Scholar Ranking Systems

### Global Rankings

#### Highly Cited Researchers (Clarivate)
- **URL**: https://clarivate.com/highly-cited-researchers/
- **Coverage**: 21 ESI fields across all disciplines
- **Update**: Annual (2025 list: 7,131 awards to 6,868 researchers)
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Industry standard)
- **Selection**: Top 1% by citations in Web of Science (11-year window)
- **Key Stats**: US 37.4%, China 19.7%, UK 6.8%
- **Access**: Free public database with filters

#### ScholarGPS Rankings
- **URL**: https://scholargps.com/
- **Coverage**: 14 fields, 177 disciplines, 35,000+ specialties
- **Update**: Annual (2025 edition available)
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (AI-powered quantitative analysis)
- **Metrics**: Productivity, Impact (excluding self-citations), Quality (h-index)
- **Top 0.05%**: "Highly Ranked Scholars" designation
- **Access**: Free public rankings (lifetime + 5-year options)

#### Research.com Rankings
- **URL**: https://research.com/scientists-rankings/
- **Coverage**: Computer Science + multiple disciplines
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (D-index methodology)
- **Data Source**: OpenAlex + CrossRef
- **Access**: Free searchable database

#### AD Scientific Index
- **URL**: https://www.adscientificindex.com/
- **Coverage**: 2.6M scientists, 13 fields, 221 disciplines
- **Metrics**: Total citations, 5-year citations, h-index
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Access**: Free rankings by field and country

### Discipline-Specific Rankings

#### CSRankings (Computer Science)
- **URL**: https://csrankings.org/
- **Coverage**: CS institutions and faculty worldwide
- **Update**: Continuous
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Metrics-based on top conferences)
- **Methodology**: Faculty publications at top-tier conferences
- **Access**: Free interactive rankings by area

#### Google Scholar
- **URL**: https://scholar.google.com/citations
- **Coverage**: All disciplines
- **Update**: Real-time
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐ (Self-reported, but comprehensive)
- **Metrics**: H-index, i10-index, citation counts
- **Access**: Free public profiles

### Chinese Scholar Rankings

#### 中国高被引学者 (Highly Cited Chinese Researchers)
- **URL**: https://www.eol.cn/shuju/paiming/
- **Coverage**: 84 first-level subjects
- **Update**: Annual (2024: 6,388 scholars)
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Scopus-based)
- **Criteria**: Top 1% global citations, FWCI > 1
- **Top Institutions**: CAS (533), Tsinghua (200+), Zhejiang (200+)
- **Access**: Free public rankings

#### CNKI Scholar Database (中国知网-学者库)
- **URL**: https://yszx.casad.cas.cn/
- **Coverage**: Chinese academic publications
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Metrics**: H-index, G-index, PCSI
- **Access**: Free with CNKI account

## 2. Award Winners & Honors

### Nobel Prize
- **URL**: https://www.nobelprize.org/
- **Categories**: Physics, Chemistry, Medicine, Literature, Peace, Economics
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Highest prestige)
- **Database**: Complete records since 1901
- **2024 Notable**: Hopfield & Hinton (Physics, ML foundations)

### Turing Award (Computer Science)
- **URL**: https://amturing.acm.org/
- **Prize**: $1 million
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ ("Nobel Prize of Computing")
- **2024**: Barto & Sutton (Reinforcement Learning)
- **Database**: Complete list from 1966-present

### Fields Medal (Mathematics)
- **URL**: https://mathworld.wolfram.com/FieldsMedal.html
- **Update**: Every 4 years
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Criteria**: Outstanding researchers under 40
- **Database**: Complete list from 1936-present

### Abel Prize (Mathematics)
- **URL**: https://www.abelprize.no/
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Prize**: ~$1 million

### Wolf Prize
- **URL**: https://www.wolffund.org.il/
- **Categories**: Agriculture, Chemistry, Math, Medicine, Physics, Arts
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

### 国家自然科学奖 (State Natural Science Award - China)
- **URL**: https://www.cas.cn/ky/kjjl/gjzrkxj/
- **Coverage**: Basic and applied research
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (China's highest science award)

- **URL**: https://awards.acm.org/fellows
- **Coverage**: Computer Science
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Top 1% of ACM members)

### IEEE Fellows
- **URL**: https://www.ieee.org/membership/fellows/
- **Coverage**: Electrical Engineering, Electronics, Computing
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Highest IEEE grade)
- **Access**: Searchable directory

### National Academy of Sciences (NAS)
- **URL**: https://www.nasonline.org/
- **Coverage**: All sciences
- **Update**: Annual (~120 new members/year)
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (US premier honor)

- **Notable**: ~200 Nobel laureates
- **Access**: Searchable directory

### Royal Society Fellows
- **URL**: https://royalsociety.org/
- **Coverage**: All sciences
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (UK premier academy)
- **Access**: Public directory

### 中国科学院院士 (CAS Academicians)
- **URL**: https://yszx.casad.cas.cn/
- **Coverage**: All scientific disciplines
- **Update**: Biennial
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (China's highest honor)

## 8. Journal Ranking Tools

### Journal Citation Reports (JCR)
- **Metric**: Impact Factor (IF)
- **Coverage**: 21,000+ journals
- **Update**: Annual (2025: June)
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Industry standard)
- **Access**: Subscription (via Web of Science)

### SCImago Journal Rank (SJR)
- **Metric**: SJR indicator
- **Coverage**: All Scopus journals
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Access**: Free (https://www.scimagojr.com/)

### Google Scholar Metrics
- **Metric**: h5-index, h5-median
- **Coverage**: All disciplines
- **Update**: Continuous
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Access**: Free

### CiteScore
- **Metric**: Citation-based
- **Coverage**: Scopus-indexed journals
- **Update**: Annual
- **Credibility**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Access**: Free

## 9. Quick Reference: Best Sources by Use Case

### Finding Top Scholars
1. **Global**: Highly Cited Researchers (Clarivate)
2. **Computer Science**: CSRankings + Google Scholar
3. **Chinese Scholars**: 中国高被引学者 + CNKI
4. **Cross-verification**: ScholarGPS + Research.com

### Finding Top Journals
1. **Impact Factor**: Journal Citation Reports (JCR)
2. **Open Access**: DOAJ + CORE
3. **Discipline-Specific**: See Section 4 tables
4. **Chinese Journals**: CNKI + Science Bulletin

### Finding Papers
1. **General**: Google Scholar + Web of Science
2. **Preprints**: arXiv + bioRxiv + medRxiv
3. **Open Access**: Unpaywall + PubMed Central
4. **Chinese**: CNKI + Baidu Scholar

### Verifying Credibility
1. **Awards**: Nobel + Turing + Fields Medal
2. **Fellowships**: NAS + Royal Society + ACM/IEEE Fellows
3. **Citations**: Highly Cited Researchers
4. **H-index**: Google Scholar + Scopus
